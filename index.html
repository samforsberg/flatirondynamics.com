First platform

A low-cost cycloidal drive without the cheap feel.

Our first product is focused on the problem most small robotics teams hit early: precision motion gets expensive fast. FD-01 is being designed to make high-ratio, compact motion more accessible without giving up stiffness, repeatability, or a clean integration path.

Precision

Reduced backlash, smoother control.

Made for arms, mobile robots, research rigs, and compact automation where sloppy motion ruins the whole system.

Cost
